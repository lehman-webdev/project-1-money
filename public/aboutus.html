About Us

We are a company that connects black owned businesses to consumers around the world.

We aim to give small an medium sized minority owned businessess a platform and the ability to connect with customers and potential partners the world over. We are more than just a directory.
It takes time and patience to build a successful business and we at Blk Biz understand that. This is the reason that we take our time to make sure to create a
platform for businesses to grow their consumer base and connect with new people that may otherwise be outside of the reach of a small business.
We are the missing link that will help businesses circulate currancy in the communities that they operate in and in turn create even more exciting business opportunites.
At Blk Biz, we also understand that if you are successful, than we are successful and because of this we do everything in our power to put
the power in the hands of the small business owners who dedicate themselves to their employees and comunitees.
