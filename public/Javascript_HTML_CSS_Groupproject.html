Biography

I want to create an Impryceless Kloset space. I am an aspiring fashion designer and artist. I love to design, create and put clothing together that is aesthetically pleasing to my eyes. I find joy in creating and making myself and other people look good. I would like to create a space where people can visit like their own personal closet to get fashion tips and styles. This Closet is for everyone every body type. The Closet will focus on my fashion ensemble or a fashion tip for any given day etc. I want the space to be interactive females can voice their opinions and fashion trails and problems they have. They can give suggestions on what look they would like to see how I could improve the site and take fashion to a new level. By simply just leaving a comment below thank you for visiting my page.
